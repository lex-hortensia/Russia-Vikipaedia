About Vikipaedia

The Vikipaedia Foundation was founded by Boris Nemtsov in 2009, with the goal of giving the Russian people a free and factual source for information on Russian and global history. After Nemtsov's sudden disappearance in February 2015, the Russian authorities claimed the site, and have since reuploaded it to preserve Nemtsov's memory and to continue his mission.
